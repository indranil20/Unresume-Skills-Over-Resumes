The Problem with Resumes

Traditional hiring is broken. Companies ask for 5 years of experience for an entry-level role. Candidates inflate their CV. Interviewers make snap decisions based on alma mater instead of ability.

The result? Great talent is invisible. Companies miss out on the best hires. And candidates spend months tailoring PDFs that no one really reads.
